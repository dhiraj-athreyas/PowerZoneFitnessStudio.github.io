Lets work on back

1. Pull ups

Pull-ups are a highly effective upper-body exercise that involves gripping an overhead bar
and lifting your body upward until your chin reaches or surpasses the bar.
They target the back, shoulders, and arms, building strength and enhancing overall fitness.

2. Deadlifts

The deadlift is a foundational strength exercise in which
a person lifts a loaded barbell or weight from the ground to a standing position.
It targets multiple muscle groups, including the back, legs, and core, making it an essential compound movement in strength training.

3. Barbell Rows

Barbell rows are a compound strength exercise that targets the upper back and lats.
By lifting a barbell from a bent-over position, they help build a powerful and well-defined back while improving overall posture and grip strength.
