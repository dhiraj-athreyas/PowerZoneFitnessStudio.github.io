Lets work on arms

1. Shoulder press

The shoulder press is a fundamental upper body exercise that targets the deltoid muscles.
It involves lifting a weight overhead from shoulder height, helping to build strength and definition in the shoulders and upper arms.

2. Diamond Press up

Diamond push-ups are a challenging variation of the classic push-up exercise.
They involve forming a diamond shape with your hands close together beneath your chest.
This targets the triceps and chest muscles, enhancing strength and definition.

3. Barbell Curls

Barbell curls are a classic strength training exercise targeting the biceps.
Using a straight barbell, you lift the weight from a fully extended arm position to a flexed bicep, building upper arm strength and definition.
