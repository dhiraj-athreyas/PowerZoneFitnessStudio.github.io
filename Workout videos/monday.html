Lets work on chest

1. Bench Press

This exercise helps to activate the entire chest muscle.
Position the bar properly on your palm to have a firm grip.

2. Dumbbell Press

The dumbbell chest press is a compound movement as it engages multiple muscle groups.
Choose proper dumbell so that you perform the exercise with proper form.

3. Parallel Bar Dips

An effective workout that involves lifting your own body weight.
This exercise helps build chest strength and definition.
While also engaging the triceps and shoulders for a well-rounded upper body workout.
